About Us

Welcome to GL Sports Club,

your premier destination for fitness and indoor sports excellence, proudly established in 2023.

At GL Sports Club, we offer state-of-the-art gym facilities and dedicated spaces for various indoor sports practices, ensuring that you have everything you need to achieve your fitness goals and hone your skills.

Our club is the brainchild of the visionary owners of MaxO Gym in Kamburupitiya, bringing their expertise and passion for fitness to create a dynamic and vibrant community.

We provide all the necessary sports equipment for a seamless and productive training experience.

Join us at GL Sports Club, where fitness meets fun, and elevate your training to the next level!

Facilities

Indoor Gym Facility

At GL Sports Club, our indoor gym facility offers everything you need for a complete workout experience, despite its cozy size. Enjoy modern equipment and the full range of amenities found in larger gyms. For personalized attention, you can also book a personal trainer to guide you through your fitness journey. Join us and take advantage of our comprehensive gym services tailored to your needs.

Chess and Caram classes

GL Sports Club collaborates to offer classes for indoor games like chess and carrom. Join through the club to enhance your skills and enjoy these engaging activities. Our expert instructors provide comprehensive lessons, ensuring a fun and educational experience. Connect with fellow enthusiasts and improve your game at GL Sports Club.

Swimming Classes

GL Sports Club offers swimming classes in collaboration with a local school pool in the Kamburupitiya area. Join these classes by contacting the trainer through the club. Our experienced instructors ensure comprehensive lessons, catering to all skill levels. Dive in and improve your swimming abilities with GL Sports Club's expert guidance.
