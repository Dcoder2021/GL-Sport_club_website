About Us

Welcome to GL Sports Club,

your premier destination for fitness and indoor sports excellence, proudly established in 2023.

At GL Sports Club, we offer state-of-the-art gym facilities and dedicated spaces for various indoor sports practices, ensuring that you have everything you need to achieve your fitness goals and hone your skills.

Our club is the brainchild of the visionary owners of MaxO Gym in Kamburupitiya, bringing their expertise and passion for fitness to create a dynamic and vibrant community.

We provide all the necessary sports equipment for a seamless and productive training experience.

Join us at GL Sports Club, where fitness meets fun, and elevate your training to the next level!
